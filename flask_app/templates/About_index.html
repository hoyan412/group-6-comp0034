The eating habits of individuals have been a persistent issue within the UK. In order to avoid health risks, it is important to understand the available dietary habits data and to have a well-balanced diet.


Goal

We aim to develop an application that allows users to clearly visualize the nutrition data and connect with like-minded people about their issues and/or interests related to health and nutrition. This application will target the UK population who are interested in health as well as facilitate the work of nutritionists and health specialists.


Information

NUTRITA is a web application created for the COMP0034 Application Programming for Data Science Module at University College London. The data collected from our users are used for educational purposes only.

The selected dataset is the "UK National Diet and Nutrition Survey (NDNS) Rolling Programme" published by the Public Health England and Food Standards Agency retrieved from the UK government website. It includes information on nutrient intake and food consumption of the UK population aged 1.5 years and over. Our visualisations focus on the intake and contribution data of the following nutrients - Protein, Fat, Free Sugar, Magnesium and Calcium.
